# Phase -1: Brand Positioning & Ecosystem Partnerships

[← Overview](00-overview.md) | [Phase 0 →](phase-00.md)

---

## Purpose

Establish thought leadership and strategic alliances to fuel executive recruitment and topic intelligence pipelines.

## Teams

Marketing, Partnerships, Content Studio.

## Tools

- Notion CMS (content planning).
- Sanity (headless CMS for syndication).
- LinkedIn API (amplification and lead gen).
- Substack (newsletter distribution).
- PartnerStack (alliance management).
- Spotify (podcast hosting and analytics).

## Process

```
Thought leadership production (research briefs, op-eds)
↓
Partner co-creation (joint webinars, whitepapers)
↓
Content syndication (LinkedIn, Forbes, industry outlets)
↓
Executive discovery (inbound inquiries, referral tracking)
```

## Deliverables

- Quarterly research reports (10–15 pages, data-backed).
- Podcast series (bi-weekly episodes, 20–30 mins).
- 5–10 partner alliances (MOUs with consultancies, VCs).
- Inbound executive leads database (segmented by industry).

## Metrics

| Metric              | Target    |
|---------------------|-----------|
| Inbound executive leads | 200+/month |
| Partner referrals  | 20+/month |
| Content engagement | >5% (views-to-clicks) |

## Success Criteria

>90% of leads convert to qualified prospects; partnerships yield 15% of total revenue.
