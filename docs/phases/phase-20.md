## Purpose

Expand capacity while experimenting with formats.

## Teams

Leadership, HR, Innovation Lab.

## Tools

- Notion (roadmap wiki).
- Figma (format prototypes).

## Process

```
Capacity audit (bottlenecks)
↓
Hiring prioritization (skills gaps)
↓
Experiment design (e.g., VR pilots)
↓
Pilot rollout and eval
```

## Deliverables

- Hiring roadmap (quarterly targets).
- Event format experiments (3–5 pilots/year).

## Metrics

| Metric             | Target    |
|--------------------|-----------|
| Headcount growth   | 20%/year  |
| Experiment success | >60%      |

## Success Criteria

Scale to 20 events/day without quality drop.
