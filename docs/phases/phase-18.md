# Phase 18: Community Flywheel Activation

[← Phase 17](phase-17.md) | [Overview](00-overview.md) | [Phase 19 →](phase-19.md)

---

## Purpose

Leverage events to spark organic growth via peer networks.

## Teams

Community, Concierge.

## Platforms

- Slack (real-time channels).
- Circle (forum-style groups).

## Process

```
Group formation (topic-based)
↓
Content seeding (event recaps)
↓
Moderation (guidelines enforcement)
↓
Referral prompts (in-app)
```

## Deliverables

- Peer advisory groups (5–10 members each).
- Executive referral program (incentivized).

## Metrics

| Metric             | Target    |
|--------------------|-----------|
| Active members     | >60%      |
| Referral volume    | 15%/month |

## Success Criteria

Community generates 20% of new executives.
