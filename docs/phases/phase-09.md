# Phase 9: Moderator Preparation

[← Phase 8](phase-08.md) | [Overview](00-overview.md) | [Phase 10 →](phase-10.md)

---

## Purpose

Equip moderators for dynamic, insightful facilitation.

## Teams

Content & Facilitation.

## Tools

- ExecuNet (moderator sourcing).
- Notion playbooks (guides).
- Zoom rehearsal (dry runs).

## Process

```
Moderator selection (expertise match)
↓
Topic deep-dive (research packet)
↓
Prompt engineering (open-ended questions)
↓
Rehearsal and feedback loop
```

## Deliverables

- Moderator briefing (1-hour video + docs).
- Discussion prompts (5–7 layered questions).

## Metrics

| Metric           | Target |
|------------------|--------|
| Moderator score  | >8/10  |

## Success Criteria

Moderators deliver >90% on-time, high-engagement sessions.
