# Phase 8: Sponsor Success Preparation

[← Phase 7](phase-07.md) | [Overview](00-overview.md) | [Phase 9 →](phase-09.md)

---

## Purpose

Prime sponsors for value extraction through targeted prep.

## Teams

Sponsor Success.

## Tools

- HubSpot (deal tracking).
- Clay (match automation).
- Affinity (network overlaps).
- Chili Piper (meeting scheduler).

## Process

```
Executive cohort review
↓
Match scoring (relevance to sponsor goals)
↓
Briefing deck assembly
↓
Intro scripting (icebreakers)
```

## Deliverables

- Sponsor briefing packet (PDF + dashboard).
- Executive match list (top 3–5 per sponsor).

## Metrics

| Metric                   | Target |
|--------------------------|--------|
| Intro opportunities/event| 3–5    |

## Success Criteria

>70% of intros lead to follow-up meetings.
