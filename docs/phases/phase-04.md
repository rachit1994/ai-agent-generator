---

## Purpose

Ensure only high-fit executives attend, via rigorous Lead Quality Assurance (LQA).

## Teams

Executive Recruitment, Lead Quality Assurance Bench.

## Tools

- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (role scouting).
- Clay (scoring automation).
- ZoomInfo (company intel).
- Lusha (phone/email validation).
- Airtable (qualification tracker).

## Qualification Process

```
Identity verification (cross-reference sources)
↓
Seniority validation (C-level+ titles)
↓
Company size validation (revenue >$500M)
↓
Topic relevance (past engagement signals)
↓
Executive Fit Score calculation
```

## Executive Fit Score

Weighted formula:  
**0.4 × Seniority (1–10)** + **0.3 × Company Revenue ($M scale)** + **0.2 × Topic Relevance (match %)** + **0.1 × Sponsor Alignment (overlap %)**  

Threshold: **>80/100** for approval.

## Deliverables

- Approved attendee cohort (10–15 per event).
- Technographic profiles (tech stack summaries).
- Sponsor battlecards (executive-sponsor match intel).

## Metrics

| Metric         | Target   |
|----------------|----------|
| ICP match rate | >90%     |
| Acceptance rate| 10–20%   |

## Success Criteria

>85% of qualified attendees become repeat members.
