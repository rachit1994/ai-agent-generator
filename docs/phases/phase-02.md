# Phase 2: Executive Lifecycle Management

[← Phase 1](phase-01.md) | [Overview](00-overview.md) | [Phase 3 →](phase-03.md)

---

## Purpose

Track and nurture executives through personalized stages to maximize long-term value.

## Lifecycle Stages

```
Prospect (inbound lead)
↓
Qualified (ICP match)
↓
Invited (personalized outreach)
↓
Approved (fit score >80)
↓
Attended Once (first event)
↓
Repeat Member (2–5 events/year)
↓
Trusted Member (6+ events, referrals)
↓
Community Leader (moderation, advocacy)
↓
Advisor (strategic input, equity ties)
```

## Teams

Executive Recruitment, Concierge, Data & AI.

## Tools

- HubSpot lifecycle tracking (stage automation).
- Amplitude engagement analytics (behavioral scoring).
- Affinity relationship graph (influence mapping).

## Process

```
Stage entry triggers (e.g., form submit → prospect)
↓
Nurture automation (drip campaigns, reminders)
↓
Engagement monitoring (clicks, attendance)
↓
Promotion criteria (e.g., NPS >8 → repeat)
↓
Retention interventions (concierge check-ins)
```

## Deliverables

- Lifecycle dashboards (real-time funnel visualization).
- Executive reputation scores (0–100, based on contributions).

## Metrics

| Metric             | Target |
|--------------------|--------|
| Executive retention| >70%   |
| Repeat attendance  | >65%   |
| NPS                | >8.5   |

## Success Criteria

>50% of prospects reach "trusted member" within 18 months; lifetime value >$5K/executive.
