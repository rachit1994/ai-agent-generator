# Phase 13: Real-Time Lead Scoring

[← Phase 12](phase-12.md) | [Overview](00-overview.md) | [Phase 14 →](phase-14.md)

---

## Purpose

Identify and alert on sponsor opportunities during/after events.

## Teams

Sponsor Success, Data Engineering.

## Tools

- HubSpot webhooks (event triggers).
- Segment (data routing).
- Amplitude (behavioral analytics).

## Process

```
Engagement capture (speaks, polls, chats)
↓
Scoring model (e.g., +10 for sponsor mention)
↓
Alert generation (Slack/email)
↓
Handover to success team
```

## Deliverables

- Engagement score report (per executive).
- Sponsor lead alerts (prioritized list).

## Metrics

| Metric          | Target    |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Time-to-meeting | <48 hrs   |

## Success Criteria

>60% of scored leads convert to intros.
