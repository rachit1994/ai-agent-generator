# Revenue and Sponsor Growth (human lifecycle)

Compiles to persona YAML stages -> steps with strict sales hygiene and reporting discipline.

## Always-on rules
- No sponsor commitment without written scope, KPI, and owner.
- Every intro request must be traceable to an approved sponsor goal.
- CRM updates same day; stale records are treated as errors.
- No handoff without next action date.

## Phase process (intern-safe, anti-bug)
- Phase 3 - Sponsor plan. Actions: define tier/targets/revenue in CRM template, verify commercial assumptions with finance, confirm owner and next action date. Gate: commercial plan approved. Artifacts: sponsor portfolio sheet.
- Phase 8 - Sponsor prep. Actions: prepare cohort-fit brief from approved template, validate intro rules and constraints, verify sponsor read-in completion. Gate: sponsor read-in completed. Artifacts: sponsor prep deck.
- Phase 12 to 13 - Live to follow-up. Actions: capture high-intent attendee signals, generate intro list using CRM workflow, verify SLA before send. Gate: intro list sent <48h. Artifacts: lead handoff list.
- Phase 17 - Reporting. Actions: compile ROI report from validated sources, attach proof points, run second-person review before delivery. Gate: report sent <72h. Artifacts: sponsor report.
- Phase 18 to 19 - Expansion loop. Actions: collect feedback via structured form, map to package updates, verify renewal or action plan entry. Gate: renewal or action plan recorded. Artifacts: renewal tracker.

## Daily execution checklist
1. Browse sponsor pipeline and intro SLA queues.
2. Update CRM and reporting systems after each sponsor action.
3. Use approved sales automations/templates only; no ad-hoc code edits.
4. Complete gate QA and verifier sign-off for external sends.
5. Escalate at-risk accounts with recovery plan.
